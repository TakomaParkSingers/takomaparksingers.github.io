About Us

The Takoma Park Singers is a community chorus whose members who live in and around Takoma Park, Maryland and share in the love of music. We began in the late 1960's and have been directed by Carole Whittam since 1981. We enjoy preparing for concerts and giving performances throughout the Washington DC area. From September to May, we rehearse on Wednesday evenings near downtown Silver Spring. Seasonal performances include a Winter Holiday Concert and a Spring Concert. Recordings of past performances can be found on our YouTube channel. We hope you will join us at a future performance!
